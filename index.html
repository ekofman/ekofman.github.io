Snow White & the Seven Million Followers

In a not-so-distant future where AI has decimated the creator economy, a mysterious new social media platform dangles a lifeline before the world’s desperate influencers, offering them one last chance to maintain their livelihoods. But is the deal too good to be true?

A sharp commentary on the intersection of social media and AI, the collapse of authenticity, and the unsettling possibility that in a world where everything might be fake, the most dangerous lies are the ones we most desperately want to believe.

Original rock opera

An original rock opera about Fritz Haber — the chemist whose work fed billions yet also fueled the worst excesses of the Great War. Though the events it depicts took place a century ago, Haber’s examination of its protagonist’s life and work raises still-relevant questions about the role scientists should play as moral stewards of scientific progress.

Staged as a concert-style reading at the Armory in Boston, 2019, to an audience of over a hundred. A few reviews from attendees:

“We were singing the lyrics after the show was over!”

“The lyrics are outstanding — intelligent, alliterative, and they do a wonderful job carrying the story.”

Currently seeking producers interested in helping develop Haber toward a full theatrical production. Reach out and let’s chat →
