Put downloadable files here — e.g. a multi-page comic PDF.

To show a "Read the comic (PDF)" tile in the Art & Cartoons gallery:
1. Drop your PDF in this folder, e.g. files/comic.pdf
2. In index.html, find the gallery__grid and uncomment the <a class="frame frame--pdf"> example (or copy it), pointing href at your file.

For single cartoon images, use PNG/JPG in the images/ folder instead — those
display directly inside the gallery frames.
